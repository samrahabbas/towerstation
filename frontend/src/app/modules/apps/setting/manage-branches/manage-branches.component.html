Did you know?
  • For each group below, you should select Shared if you want to share each item in that group amongst all the users in your company. This will help you prevent duplicate items from being created and allow for universal reporting across your company. This option is useful, for example, if the different branches / departments in your company share locations that they pick-up or deliver to.
  • Alternatively, User Choice should be used if you want to allow your users to choose to add items to any of their own branches / departments, including Shared. This allows your users to keep their data within their own silo, either to help declutter the system, or to prevent others from viewing protected or sensitive information.
  • Provided you have added yourself to all branches, as an Administrator, you are able to view and modify any data entered in TowerStation regardless of branch. In addition, you have the added ability to switch the branches / departments that any individual item or user belongs to at any time, regardless of the settings below.
